## FAQ

**Q: Is Triva truly free?**  
A: Yes! Triva is MIT licensed and always will be free to use.

**Q: What if I can't afford to sponsor?**  
A: No problem! Contributing code, docs, or support is equally valuable.

**Q: Can I sponsor anonymously?**  
A: Yes, most platforms offer anonymous sponsorship options.

**Q: Are donations tax-deductible?**  
A: Donations through Open Collective are tax-deductible in the US. GitHub Sponsors depends on your location.

**Q: How do I cancel my sponsorship?**  
A: You can cancel anytime through GitHub Sponsors or Open Collective.

**Q: Can I sponsor specific features?**  
A: Gold and Platinum sponsors can request specific features. Contact us to discuss.

**Q: How often are financial reports published?**  
A: Quarterly, available on Open Collective.
